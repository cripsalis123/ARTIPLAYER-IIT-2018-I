The objective of this project is to achieve entrepreneurship and improvement skills with innovation tools, offering a product aimed at a community that is currently developing and growing, the videogame community, being more specific, to the DOTA 2 community. It seeks to offer articles and everyday objects with the theme of the game to its users, we saw a good marketing opportunity in the video game scene that is practically being ignored today, for us to innovate with the project. Applying different methodologies and study techniques for the production of the product.

About Us

We are currently students of the Industrial Electrotechnics career (4th semester) at the TECSUP Higher Institute, with knowledge in the field of electricity and the use of it in the different daily tasks, whether work or academic, in the course of academic preparation. learned to use different applications and design programs that will serve us for our project, for example: AutoCad, Rhinceros, Solid Work, among others. We consider that we have the attitude and motivation necessary to carry out this project.
